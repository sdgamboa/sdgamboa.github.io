Ph.D. in Biological Sciences

Yucatan Center for Scientific Research, Mexico

Welcome to my website!

I’m interested in the analysis of genomic data to understand the biological world. I have experience in the implementation of different bioinformatics pipelines for evolutionary analyses and transcriptomics.

My last affiliation was to the laboratory of Dr. Luis C. Rodríguez-Zapata at the Yucatan Center for Scientific Research, Mexico, where I studied abiotic stress tolerance mechanisms in plants.
